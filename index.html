How to choose the best hypertrophy program for you

Pick a program based on your current experience, goals, and preferences. Ensure that progressive overload is incorporated for muscle growth.

  • Beginners: Focus on a program with compound lifts and moderate volume, like StrongLifts 5x5.
  • Advanced: Programs with higher volume and periodization, such as P.H.A.T.
  • Customization: Adapt programs like 5/3/1 to include hypertrophy days and volume adjustments.
